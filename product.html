    GFRP Rebar

    Glass Fibre Reinforced Polymer Rebar is a modern construction composite rebar and a substitute for steel rebar.

    GFRP Rebar offers superior performance with corrosion resistance, making it two times stronger and ¼ lighter than traditional steel bars. Unlike steel rebars, GFRP rebars do not rust and are compatible with water and chemicals, making construction lighter and stronger.
